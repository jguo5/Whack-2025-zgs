Resources

    Further Information on Climate Change

  • The US Climate Vulnerability Index

    • Interactive tool to help visualize which communities face the greatest challenges from the impacts of a changing climate.

  • Sea Level Rise Viewer

    • Sea level rise viewer map of the USA to help learn about which areas may be impacted due to rising sea levels.

    What to Do if You are Affected

  • US Climate Resilience Toolkit

    • Climate resilience toolkits with specific details on what to do if your region has experienced a negative impact due to climate-related hazards. These toolkits help communities to assess climate-related risks, prioritize vulnerabilities, and implement adaptive strategies to protect people, property, and natural resources. It guides stakeholders through understanding hazards, evaluating options, planning actions, and iteratively monitoring outcomes to build long-term resilience.

  • Managed Retreat Toolkit

    • A website that discusses “Managed retreat”, which is the voluntary movement of people away from vulnerable coastal areas. It includes a toolkit that has multiple legal and policy tools for areas that are looking to perform managed retreat. Provides a comprehensive approach to managing the complexities of managed retreats; includes a section on the legal framework, planning tools, infrastructure, acquisition tools, regulatory tools, market-based tools, and cross-cutting policy considerations.

  • Hazard Mitigation Policy Guide

    • Focuses on potential policy outcomes as divided into interagency, regional, and local planning capacity and cooperation; interrelationships between plans, development codes, and ordinances; resiliency standards; incentives; stakeholder involvement and engagement; and specific hazard types.
